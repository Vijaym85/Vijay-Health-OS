  VIJAY HEALTH OS — PWA Deployment Guide
═══════════════════════════════════════════════

WHAT THIS IS
────────────
A Progressive Web App (PWA) — a website that
installs on your phone exactly like a native app.
Works offline. Sends real phone notifications.
Saves all your data permanently.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════
STEP 1 — DEPLOY TO NETLIFY (5 minutes, FREE)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════

1. Go to https://www.netlify.com
2. Click "Sign up" → use Google or email
3. On your dashboard, find the box that says:
   "Drag and drop your site folder here"
4. Drag the entire "vijay-health-pwa" folder
   into that box
5. Wait ~30 seconds
6. Netlify gives you a URL like:
   https://amazing-name-123.netlify.app

That's your permanent app URL!
(Optional: rename it to something like
 https://vijay-health.netlify.app in Site Settings)

═══════════════════════════════════════════════
STEP 2 — ADD TO HOME SCREEN
═══════════════════════════════════════════════

ON ANDROID (Chrome):
1. Open your Netlify URL in Chrome
2. Tap the ⋮ menu (top right)
3. Tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. Tap "Add"
→ App icon appears on home screen!

ON iPHONE (Safari):
1. Open your Netlify URL in Safari
   (MUST be Safari, not Chrome)
2. Tap the Share button (box with arrow ↑)
3. Scroll down → tap "Add to Home Screen"
4. Tap "Add"
→ App icon appears on home screen!

═══════════════════════════════════════════════
STEP 3 — ENABLE REMINDERS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════

1. Open the app from your home screen
2. A notification prompt appears at the bottom
3. Tap "Enable Reminders"
4. Tap "Allow" when your phone asks

→ You'll now get phone alerts at:
   • 6:00 AM  — Wake Up
   • 7:15 AM  — Breakfast
   • 10:00 AM — Sugar Tabs 💊
   • 11:00 AM — Fruit Time
   • 1:00 PM  — Lunch
   • 4:30 PM  — Protein Intake
   • 7:00 PM  — Gym
   • 8:30 PM  — Dinner
   ... and every other scheduled slot

NOTE: For notifications to fire throughout
the day, open the app once each morning.
This schedules the day's reminders.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════
HOW TO USE THE APP
═══════════════════════════════════════════════

📅 SCHEDULE TAB
• Tap ● circle to mark any item DONE
• Tap ✏️ pencil to edit time / label / detail
• Use the gym toggle to switch between
  Morning (6:30 AM) and Evening (7:00 PM)
• Your edits save permanently

🥗 FOOD LOG TAB
• Select the meal slot at the top
• Browse or search 41 Indian veg foods
• Tap + to add any food
• Use − / + to adjust quantity
• Add custom foods with your own calories
• All data saves and persists daily

📊 SUMMARY TAB
• See total calories vs 1,800 kcal goal
• Protein / Carbs / Fat progress
• Schedule completion tracker
• Your health targets at a glance
